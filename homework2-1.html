Programming Exercise 2.1 LOOPS

Remember the moving image example you did for HW#2? Now, you need to do the same thing for multiple images (at least 4) using for loop. It should look like a continuous film strip wrapping around. This time you need to also add two buttons to control the speed of your moving image strip, one button for increasing the speed and the other for decreasing.

You need to create 3 functions

The 1st function will be a static display of your images in a strip and should be triggered as soon as the page loads.

The 2nd function will allow the motion of your images and should be called back over and over.

The 3rd function will allow you to adjust the speed of your moving film strip and will be attached to your buttons.
